Allocate Customer Cash

The following changes have been made to Allocate Customer Cash.

Analysis

It is now possible to write off small differences between the invoiced and received amounts by posting the difference to the General Ledger.

On the Analysis Lines entry screen, the Ledger drop-down has a new entry: GL. This allows you to enter a GL account to which the balance of customer cash is posted.

Analysis Lines Ledger Drop-Down

When the new CB parameter LVCCPACB is set to Yes, if you enter a or analysis line but do not fully allocate it to invoices (or certificates), displays a warning when you leave the allocation screen (see Figure 2: Receipt Must be Fully Allocated Warning), and prevents you from posting the allocation batch.

Receipt Must be Fully Allocated Warning

When the new CB parameter PARTALOC is set to Yes, if you post an allocation batch with unallocated cash, now displays a warning (see Figure 1: Customer Cash Not Fully Allocated Warning), and creates a new Customer Cash record equal to the unallocated amount.
