Changing Your Account Image and Display Name

The Page Banner shows the image associated with your account (if one has been set up), and your user name. You can change the image, and the name that is shown on the page header.

To change your account image or display name

  1. Move the mouse pointer over the account image.
  2. From the user menu, click Change Profile.

  3. displays a Change Profile box.

    Screenshot: Change Profile 

  4. Type the name you want to display on the page header.
  5. Click the Upload Picture button and browse for a new account image.

    NOTE

    The image must be a JPG file, and ideally should be approximately 60px by 60px. You can use larger images but the browser will resize them, which does not always produce good results.
