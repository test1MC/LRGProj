Multiple Screen or Tab Access

You may find having various tabs or additional windows open for the different modules or companies may be more effective when using . This does not utile an additional and you can switch to the different views.

You may have a report or you use frequently and therefore may wish to have this available in an additional window, enabling you to continue working without losing the data. For example, look up the details of a subcontractor record whilst reviewing the subcontract ledger payments enquiry.

  1. Locate the Menu option or Hyperlink you require. Right click on the information line.
  2. The Sub Menu options will be displayed
  3. You can select to open the new information in a new tab or new window. Select the option you require.

This is a New Tab Option

The New Window Option

An alternative option to open a new window would be to select and hold the Ctrl key and select the N key. This will open the New Window displaying the Home Page of the current module you are using.

 

Note

It is recommended you close all additional tabs and windows before you log off.
