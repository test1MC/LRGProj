Mail Assistant - What the Buttons Do

Mail Assistant opens in a panel to the right when you are composing an email, and opens within the email when you are reading it.

This is what the buttons mean:

Buttons for Composing an Email

Buttons for Reading an Email

Buttons on a Record

  1. People - this allows you to find contacts in that are associated with the email addresses on the email.
  2. Search - this allows you to find records in workbenches.
  3. Regarding - this shows the records that the email has been uploaded to.
  4. Select Company - this allows you to select the company.
  5. Log Out - this logs you out of Mail Assistant.
  6. Templates - this allows you to use a email template for your message, based on the data in the record.
  7. Open in - this opens the record in .
  8. Upload - this uploads the email message to the record in .
