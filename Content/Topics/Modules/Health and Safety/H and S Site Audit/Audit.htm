Audit Workbenches – Audit Tab

The Audit tab provides a single page view of the audit form for quick entry and viewing.

The update is designed to use minimum screen space so fields are hidden until required.  Figure 1: Site Audit Form below shows a Site Audit form.  The Key Performance Indicators section uses elements to repeat multiple times, and the second element “1.2 Occupation Health – Hand Arm Vibration” is using a couple of lines.

Site Audit Form

Figure 2: Side Audit Form - Expanded shows how this can expand if the use chooses to add a comment, workflows, and if the score results in the checklist appearing.  As workflows are populated another appears on the line below up to the specified maximum for the section.
