PT/PTMMAILT - 'To' email address when emailing Meetings

This parameter is used to default the "To" email recipients when emailing Meetings documents.

Options are as follows:

  • From – use email address from the "Co-Ordinator" person on the Meetings.
  • To – use email address from the "Attendees" persons on the Meetings.
  • User – use the email address from the current user.

Multiple options can be selected.
