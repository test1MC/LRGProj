PO/HBSPRPLT - HB Subcon Price Chk Completing Plot Scheds

When completing plot schedules, what action do you want to take if some of the schedule lines do not have prices?

E = Error. prevents you from completing the schedule.

W = Warning. warns you that not all lines have prices, and asks if you want to complete the schedule.

N = No error. does not check for lines with no price.
