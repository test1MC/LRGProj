PR/PRSISHARE - Share-based pay items included in PRSIable pay

This specifies share-based remuneration pay items included in the PRSI-able pay (which is the employee PRSI-able pay).

These item amounts will be deducted from the PRSI-able pay (along with the 50% relief on pension items in the PRSIPENS parameter as introduced at the start of 2011) when calculating the employer's PRSI contribution.
