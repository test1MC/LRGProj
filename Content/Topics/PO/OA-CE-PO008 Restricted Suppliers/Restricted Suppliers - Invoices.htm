Invoices and Payments

If you enter an invoice that relates to a material or order and the PL account is overridden, will check that the supplier is a valid supplier from the 's restricted supplier list.

If you enter a manual or Subcontract Ledger payment for a supplier or subcontractor, and leave any part of the payment unallocated against a that has a restricted supplier or subcontractor list, will check that the supplier or subcontractor is a valid company from the restricted list.

Similarly, during Apply Payments entry, if you leave any part of the payment unallocated against a that has a restricted supplier or subcontractor list, will check that the supplier or subcontractor is a valid company from the restricted list.

If you enter through cash book for either a or Subcontract Ledger account, and select a on an analysis line, will check that the supplier or subcontractor is valid for that , and if you leave any part of the payment unallocated against a that has a restricted supplier or subcontractor list, will check that the supplier or subcontractor is a valid company from the restricted list.
