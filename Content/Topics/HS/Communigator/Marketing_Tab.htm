Workbench Marketing Tab

If the new Communigator parameter APILAND is set, a new ‘Marketing’ tab appears on the Workbench. This shows details of the ‘s web site activity and marketing interactions. An equivalent Marketing tab has also been added to the Customer Workbench to show details for customers.

The Marketing tab consists of three sub-tabs.

Summary

This tab shows a summary of the ‘s marketing interactions. Gauges show the percentage of emails that the has opened (Opens), the number of links in emails that they have clicked (Clicks), and the ratio of Opens to Clicks.

Web Site Activity

This tab shows a browse of the web site activity captured by Communigator.

NOTE

You need to be subscribed to the GatorLeads service for this to work.

Campaign Interactions

This tab shows a browse of the Communigator campaigns for the and the interactions the person has had with each campaign.
