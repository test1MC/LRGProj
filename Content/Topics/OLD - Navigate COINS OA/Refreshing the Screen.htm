Refreshing the Screen

You may need to refresh a screen you are currently viewing, or you have open in a separate window or tab. For example, if you have an Enquiry or Browse screen and records have input or amended, you may wish to refresh the list to show the new data, without affecting the filters.

  1. Locate the screen you wish to refresh. Right click on the screen.
  2. The Sub Menu options will be displayed
  3. Select the Refresh option. The screen will automatically refresh the data, with all the filters and search criteria.
  4. Some screens will have the Refresh button available at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Internet Explorer also has a Refresh button. This is next to the Address field. This will refresh the whole screen and remove any filters and search criteria you have entered.
