Opening Mail Assistant

The way to open Mail Assistant is different depending on whether you are using a desktop-based version of Outlook, or Office 365, and on whether you are composing an email or reading a received email.

Note: the  Mail Assistant button may have a different name, as it can be configured by your system administrator.

  1. If you are composing an email in Office 365:
    1. Click the Add-Ins button.

    2. Click the Mail Assistant button.

  2. If you are composing an email in Outlook, click the  Mail Assistant button in the message's ribbon:

  3. If you are reading an email in Office 365, in the email message, click Mail Assistant.

    Mail Assistant opens in a frame within the message.

  4. If you are reading an email in Outlook, click the  Mail Assistant button in the body of the message (shown as " Staging" in this example):

    Mail Assistant opens in a frame within the message.

  5. If you have not logged in to , log in to your account, using your normal ID and password.
