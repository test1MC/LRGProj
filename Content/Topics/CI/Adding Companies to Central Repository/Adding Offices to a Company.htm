Adding Offices to a Company

Companies can have many offices using the One to Many ratio, i.e. only one record for the Company and many office records.

You should not have the same company entered in the system more than once and therefore each office becomes an additional record against the main company record. It is on the office that the order is placed rather than the head office, so we recommend that you have all the offices entered into the system.

When adding an office, you will need to locate the company on the Company Information Workbench.

Using the Company Name hyper-link in the Name column drill down into the Company Summary screen. The Company Summary screen is displayed with various tabs for the information available.

 

Select the Offices Tab to view a listing of offices relating to the company.

To create new offices click

The Offices - Add screen is displayed to enter the details of the new office. The fields are grouped into different panels and tabs for grouping information together.

 

The Header record will display the Company name in the Company field and also if any documents are attached. This will be displayed constantly as you go through the tabs.

The Office name and type is displayed in a separate panel and will be also be available as you go through the tabs.
