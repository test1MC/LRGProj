Adding a New Job Card

 

Navigate to Plant Control > Workshop > Job Cards.

The Job cards page provide a list of all existing Job Cards.

To create a new Job card, click

The screen is split into three areas: Detail, Recharge and Completion. When setting up a new Job Card, only the Detail and Recharge sections should be completed.

DO NOT fill in the Completion section until the work is actually complete.

Detail Fields

Field

Description
Workshop Enter the stock location, depot, or workshop where the asset is kept.
Asset Enter a code for the asset (a lookup is available)
Event Select an Event Code (e.g. Repair, Service etc.)
Title The Title of this Workshop Job
Reg. No The registration Number (if relevant to this workshop job)
Meter Reading The meter reading (if relevant to this workshop job)
Date The date of the workshop job.
Order Enter the Customer Order Number, if relevant. May be left blank.
Received Enter the date received (optional)
Required Enter the required date (optional)
Authorised Enter the person who authorised the Workshop Job Card (optional)
Estimate Enter the estimated cost (optional)
Reference Enter a reference for the estimate (optional)
Description Enter brief details of the work to be done

Recharge Fields

Field

Description
Recharge

Whether costs to this Workshop Job Card get recharged. This field will initially be ticked. You will not be able to un-tick it until the Details section has been completed.

If ticked, the following fields are available for completion
Charged Whether this Workshop Job Card has been fully charged
Ready to Charge Whether the workshop job card is ready to charge
Recharge To The type of analysis (customer, job, or split). Selecting the analysis will allow the correct lookup in the adjacent field.
Charge Centre The charge centre. A lookup is available.
Description The recharging description.

Click

The Job Card is now ready to accept costs. These costs can come from different sources, namely:

  • Stock Issues
  • Workshop Orders
  • Payroll
  • Journals
